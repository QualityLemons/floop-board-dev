Welcome to the future of good feedback

What is Floop?

Floop is a tool created by a frustrated activity designer to support children, young people and young adults to give feedback at their own pace.

Why use Floop?

Floop provides a unique and engaging way for users to express their thoughts and feelings through creative drawing.

How is my feedback used?

Your feedback is valuable and will be used to improve the Floop tool and enhance the user experience for everyone.
