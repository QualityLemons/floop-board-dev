Floop!

Creative Feedback Tool

What is Floop!?!

The goal of this project is to create a simple web application that allows users to submit feedback in the form of a drawing. Users can enter their postcode and draw their feedback on a canvas, which will help us gather insights in a fun and interactive way. This project is built using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and is designed to be responsive and user-friendly.

🚀

How does Floop! work?

It's really simple. All you need is a UK postcode and your imagination. No matter how you are feeling, feel free to draw a picture or write a word or do anything you like to express your feedback. Once you submit, your drawing will be sent to our server for analysis and storage. Once you submit your drawing, you, a friend, or an activity provider can view it and add their own image as a comment.

📱

Is Floop! Mobile-Friendly?

We hope so! The application is designed to be fully responsive and works well on both desktop and mobile devices.

🛡️

Floop! respects your privacy

You don't need to supply an email address to use Floop!.
