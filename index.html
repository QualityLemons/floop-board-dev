Floop!

Welcome to the future of good feedback

Floop is a tool created by a frustrated activity designer to support children, young people and young adults to give feedback at their own pace.

How does it work?

Floop is a simple web-based tool that allows users to draw and write feedback on a canvas. It is designed to be intuitive and easy to use, making it accessible for people of all ages and abilities.

Why use Floop?

Floop provides a unique and engaging way for users to express their thoughts and feelings. It can be used in a variety of settings, such as classrooms, workshops, and therapy sessions, to encourage open communication and foster creativity.

How is my feedback used?

Your feedback is valuable and will be used to improve the Floop tool and enhance the user experience. We may also share aggregated feedback with our community to inspire new features and improvements.
