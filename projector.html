ProjectorFeedback Tools

Projector is a tool designed to help users quickly turn their ideas into simple, visual projects that can be easily shared with others.

About the Project

The Mission

Traditional feedback forms can be intimidating. This sandbox explores modular "micro-interactions" that make giving feedback feel like a natural part of a conversation.

Design Principles

  • Low cognitive load
  • Mobile-first interaction
  • Accessible and inclusive design
